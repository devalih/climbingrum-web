Salamah Alzalabia

ENSA Certified Mountain Guide

🏔️ Local Heritage & Modern Expertise

Born and raised in Wadi Rum village, Salamah represents the perfect fusion of ancestral desert knowledge and modern mountaineering techniques. As a certified guide from ENSA (École Nationale de Ski et d'Alpinisme), he brings internationally recognized training standards to the ancient landscapes of his homeland.

Reportedly the most experienced guide in Rum village, Salamah learned the desert's secrets from his ancestors while mastering contemporary climbing and safety protocols through rigorous professional training.

Ali Hussein

ENSA Certified Mountain & Canyon Guide

🌍 International Experience & Innovation

From Amman, Ali brings a modern, international perspective to Wadi Rum climbing. As an ENSA-certified mountain and canyon guide, he has climbed extensively across the USA, Europe, and Asia, bringing world-class techniques and safety standards to Jordan\'s desert landscapes.

Ali is a route developer and holds the first ascent of "Crazy Camel," considered the best multi-pitch sport route in Wadi Rum. His innovative approach and international experience make him the perfect guide for climbers seeking cutting-edge adventures.
