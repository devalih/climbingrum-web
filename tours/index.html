Day 1

The Northern Circuit

Your journey begins at the Rest House, where you'll board a 4x4 to explore the iconic northern landscapes of Wadi Rum, filled with ancient history and breathtaking natural formations.

Lawrence's Spring

Stop at Lawrence's spring, see Thamudic inscriptions, and take a 10-minute easy climb to the spring for a nice view.

Red Sand Dune

Enjoy walking barefoot on the soft, warm sand and a breathtaking view of Wadi Rum's main canyon.

Khazali Canyon

One of the most beautiful spots in Rum, a shady, lush canyon full of water, inscriptions, and history.

Little Bridge

One of the most scenic spots in Rum. It's a short, easy climb to this beautiful little natural rock bridge.

Lawrence's House

A historical spot where Lawrence of Arabia lived in Rum. Offers a nice view of the surrounding area.

Burdah Bridge Viewpoint

Stop to watch and appreciate the beauty of Burdah Bridge, one of the most famous rock arches in the area.

Day 2

Southern Vistas & Hikes

The second day takes you to the serene south, offering stunning panoramic views, a rewarding hike to Jabal Hash, and famous movie locations.

Abu Khashaba Canyon

Explore the narrow, sandy passage of Abu Khashaba, which requires a short walk through the gorge.

Um Fruth Rock Bridge

A stunning natural rock arch that is much larger than the Little Bridge, offering great photo opportunities.

Jabal Hash Hike (1,701m)

A rewarding 2-hour hike to the top of Jabal Hash, providing panoramic views over the vast Wadi Sabbit.

South Rum Filming Location

Visit the area used for filming famous 'Martian' scenes, with its unique, flat, and wide landscape.

Chicken Rock

A fascinating rock formation that, depending on the angle, resembles a chicken or a pharaoh's head.

Sunset Viewpoint

The final stop, offering an unforgettable view as the sun sets over the Wadi Rum desert.
