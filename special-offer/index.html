Frequently Asked Questions

What skill level do I need?

This package is suitable for all levels, from complete beginners to experienced climbers. Our guides will select routes appropriate for your ability.

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

No! All climbing equipment (ropes, harnesses, shoes, helmets) is provided. Just bring comfortable clothes and enthusiasm!

Can I extend my climbing time?

Yes! Additional climbing hours can be added for 25 JD per hour. Just let us know when booking.

What's included in the meals?

Dinner is traditional Bedouin zarb (meat and vegetables cooked underground), and breakfast includes eggs, bread, cheese, jam, and Bedouin tea. Vegetarian options available.

How do I book?

Contact us via WhatsApp (+962 77 535 5605) or email (info@climbingrum.com) with your preferred dates and number of people.
