Authentic Bedouin Climbing Guides in Wadi Rum

Welcome to Climbing Rum, your gateway to authentic climbing adventures in Wadi Rum, Jordan. We are a 100% Jordanian-owned and operated climbing company, run by local Bedouin guides who have called this magnificent desert home for generations. Our deep connection to the land and intimate knowledge of every rock formation makes us uniquely qualified to guide your Wadi Rum climbing experience. Learn about our climbing history â†’

Wadi Rum, also known as the Valley of the Moon, is one of the world's premier climbing destinations. Our sandstone mountains offer everything from beginner-friendly sport climbing routes to challenging multi-pitch traditional climbs. Whether you're looking for single-day climbing adventures, multi-day mountain expeditions, or scenic 4x4 desert tours, our certified guides combine traditional Bedouin wisdom with modern safety standards to ensure an unforgettable experience.

What sets us apart is our commitment to authenticity and safety. All our guides are certified climbing instructors with wilderness first aid training and rescue experience. We keep our groups small-typically 2 to 6 people-to ensure personalized attention and maintain the highest safety standards. When you climb with us, you're not just hiring a guide; you're gaining a local friend who will share stories, tea, and the true spirit of Bedouin hospitality.
